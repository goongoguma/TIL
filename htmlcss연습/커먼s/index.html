ABOUT US

Coliving at COMMONTOWN

Experience co-living with commontown, where we take you beyond the limits of traditional living spaces. By breaking physical borders, we connect our members and engender community on a whole new level. Outside your private space, enjoy seamless access to artfully designed and carefully curated common spaces with commontown. Spaces catering to different lifestyles and interests come together in our towns, with commontown.

We believe that the world should be more interactive and interconnected. We believe that everyone needs community, in whatever form. We believe that in today's world, more than ever before, community is always close by. Allow us to bring it even closer, and build the community around you in our towns. Enjoy the peace and comfort of your private space. When you desire, open your door and your community is a single step away.

Come live with us in commontown where you will never feel alone. Life is better together.

Borderless coliving

Serviced house

Focus on living and leave the rest to us. We provide conveniences and complimentary services such as weekly cleaning, residential maintenance and stocking of basic supplies such as toilet paper, dishes and lightbulbs as part of your all inclusive membership.

Fully Furnished

Our houses are thoughtfully designed and furnished with quality pieces from exceptional brands, ready for you to move right in. Our kitches are outfitted with high-end appliances, dishware, and other essentials. All you have to do is show up!

Private room

Your fully furnished private space comes with a bed, side desk, wardrobe, lamp, waste bin, and full high- speed WiFi coverage. A sanctuary for you to recharge and heal in absolute privacy, when you do not wish to interact in our lovingly designed common spaces.

Flexible rental arrangement

Get a taste of COMMONTOWN life with flexible rent plans starting from a mere 3-month commitment. You can also transfer to another house within COMMONTOWN whenever you want.
